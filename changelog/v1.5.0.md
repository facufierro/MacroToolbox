# Hotkey Manager v1.5.0

## Changes

- The global scope is now a permanent, always-active fixture: it is pinned separately at the top of the sidebar and scope grid, set apart from your regular scopes, and can no longer be deleted.
- The global scope is now always on: it has no activate/deactivate button and runs in its own process from app launch until you quit, so its hotkeys work at startup and stay active alongside whichever per-app scope is running. Switching its profile takes effect immediately.
- The app window now opens maximized.
